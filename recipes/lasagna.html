Lasagna recipe!

Obviously well made it lasagna for a very formal office potlock

This lasagna its a perfect example of how you do not make a good lasagna, if you want to cook a perfect lasagna you will need the following ingredients.

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • ¾ pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 12 lasagna noodles
